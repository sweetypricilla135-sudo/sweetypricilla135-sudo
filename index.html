About Me

I design first.
Then I build it.

I’m a Computer Science student at SCAD College of Engineering who sits at the intersection of design and development. My process starts in research — understanding real user behaviour, mapping friction, and forming a clear design direction before I open Figma or write a single line of code.

What separates my work is that I don’t hand off designs and walk away. I implement them. Whether that means building a React interface from my own wireframes, integrating AI APIs into a product, or scripting automation workflows with n8n — I take ownership through to delivery.

Currently applying this as a Product Developer Intern at Genlab, and previously contributed to LLM dataset workflows at DataPattern. I’m actively exploring Agentic AI and prompt engineering as design-adjacent superpowers.

Product Design · Full-Stack Dev · PRD
PageFlip — Gen Z E-Library Platform

A research-led digital reading platform designed for how Gen Z actually consumes content — then fully built with a modern tech stack. Not a concept. A shipped product with a living PRD.

Problem
Existing e-libraries feel static, have poor search, and don’t support audio or immersive reading.
Solution
3D page-flip UI, AI-powered audio mode, typo-tolerant smart search, one-click bookmarking.
  • Full UX research: Gen Z reading behaviours, pain points, and journey maps
  • AI Listen Mode (AWS Polly) converting text to natural-sounding audio
  • Immersive 3D book interface built with Three.js and Framer Motion
  • Algolia-powered fuzzy search with real-time suggestions
  • Complete PRD: user flows, success metrics, and product roadmap

Hospital Management System

End-to-end UX design for a healthcare management platform. Every screen was informed by user research with healthcare staff, refined through usability testing, and designed to reduce cognitive load under pressure.

Problem

Solution
Unified dashboard with role-based views, clear data hierarchy, and streamlined registration-to-discharge workflows.
  • User research: persona creation, journey mapping for staff and patients
  • Role-based dashboards: receptionist, nurse, admin, and doctor views
  • Queue management, bed occupancy, vitals tracking, and reporting screens
  • Accessibility-first design with colour-coded status and clear hierarchy
